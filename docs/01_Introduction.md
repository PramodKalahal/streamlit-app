# Project Title: CareerGraph AI: Industry Skill Intelligence & Interview Readiness

## 1. Problem Statement

In the rapidly evolving job market, there is a significant disconnect between the skills students acquire in academic institutions and the skills demanded by the industry. This "Skill Gap" leads to unemployment for graduates and talent shortages for companies. 

Traditional methods of curriculum updating are slow, and students often lack real-time insights into emerging trends. There is a lack of a centralized, data-driven system that can:
- Aggregate fragmented job market data.
- Analyze real-time demand for specific technical and soft skills.
- Forecast future skill requirements.
- Provide personalized career guidance based on data rather than intuition.

**The core problem addresses:** How can we leverage Data Analytics and AI to bridge the gap between academic preparation and industry requirements?

## 2. Objectives

The primary objective is to develop an intelligent system that acts as a bridge between job seekers and the job market using advanced analytics and machine learning.

**Specific Objectives:**
1.  **Data Collection & Aggregation:** To collect and consolidate job market data (job roles, required skills, descriptions, salaries) from various sources.
2.  **Skill & Trend Analysis:** To perform descriptive analytics to identify currently trending skills and technologies.
3.  **Skill Gap Identification:** To use diagnostic analytics to compare a user's current skill set against market demands and pinpoint specific deficiencies.
4.  **Future Demand Forecasting:** To utilize predictive analytics (Machine Learning) to forecast the demand for specific skills and salary trends over the next few years.
5.  **Personalized Recommendations:** To provide prescriptive analytics in the form of career path recommendations and suggested skill upgrades.
6.  **Visualization:** To present actionable insights through an interactive dashboard for intuitive understanding by students, faculty, and recruiters.
